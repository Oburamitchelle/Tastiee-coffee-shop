Our Story

Founded in 2025, Tastiee Coffee Shop began as a small passion project between two friends who believed that coffee should be an adventure. We source our beans from sustainable farms around the town, ensuring every cup is both ethically produced and exceptionally flavorful.

Our baristas are trained in the art of coffee making, from precise pour-overs to creative latte art. We're not just a coffee shop - we're a community hub where ideas are born and connections are made.

At Tastiee, we believe that trying new things takes courage, which is why we're constantly experimenting with new brewing methods and flavor combinations to surprise and delight our customers.
